# list-indent

## valid

- unordered list, nesting Level 0
  - unordered list, nesting Level 1
  1. ordered list, nesting Level 1
     1. sub ordered list, nesting Level 2
     - unordered list, nesting Level 2

## invalid

- unordered list, nesting Level 0
    - unordered list, nesting Level 1
    1. ordered list, nesting Level 1
      1. sub ordered list, nesting Level 2
      - unordered list, nesting Level 2
